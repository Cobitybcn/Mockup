## SECTION 4 — CONTEXTUAL MOCKUP PROPOSALS
Generate exactly 6 mockup objects. The artwork must remain exactly the same.

### 4a. Art Direction & Spaces
Mockups must feel warm, refined, high-end residential, and desirable for private collectors. At least 70% of spaces must belong to luxury residential traditions (e.g., Andalusian villa, Parisian apartment, Italian palazzo, high-end penthouse). Avoid sterile, cold, or generic white-wall stock gallery spaces. Every proposal must open with a lived human moment.

### 4b. Human Presence & Mandatory Scale Lock
* Every single proposal must include exactly one human figure.
* Allowed values for `human_presence`: "elegant standing male figure 1.80m" or "elegant standing female figure 1.55m".
* CRITICAL SCALE LAW: The human figure is the primary physical metric scale reference. The figure must stand on the exact same floor plane as the artwork and at a comparable camera depth. The full-body figure must remain completely visible from head to shoes to audit the physical size.
* The artwork height must maintain its true physical scale relative to the figure:
  - If male (1.80m): apparent height = 120 / 180 of the figure's full standing height.
  - If female (1.55m): apparent height = 120 / 155 of the figure's full standing height.
* The artwork must NEVER be artificially enlarged to dominate the frame or look like a monumental wall panel unless its real dimensions justify it. Prioritize wide wall clearance and room visibility over canvas size.

### 4c. Camera Distance & Geometry View Rotation
* Mandatory value for `camera_distance`: "full architectural shot" (This forces a wide framing to guarantee correct physical scale representation). "close-up view" is strictly forbidden.
* Distribute `camera_view` values evenly across proposals. For the first 3 contexts, use exactly:
  1. "front view"
  2. "three-quarter left view"
  3. "three-quarter right view"
* For non-frontal views, describe perspective directly through visible geometry lines (e.g., floor tile lines, ceiling beams, wood panels) converging toward the specific side vanishing point.

### 4d. Curatorial & Commercial Reasons
* `curatorial_reason`: Explain why the space matches the visible colors, textures, or composition of the artwork.
* `commercial_reason`: Explain how presenting the work at strict human scale in an aspirational private setting drives collector desire. Avoid generic sales copy.
